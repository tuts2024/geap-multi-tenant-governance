STATEMENT OF WORK: Beta_Vendor_SOW_2026
This Statement of Work is entered into as of March 1, 2026, by and between Beta Corporation ("Beta") and TechSolutions Inc. ("Vendor").

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Vendor shall provide cloud migration, database architecture optimization, and infrastructure support services.

2. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Deliverables created under this SOW will be jointly owned by Beta Corporation and TechSolutions Inc. Both parties will have a perpetual, royalty-free, worldwide license to use, modify, and distribute the deliverables.

3. INDEMNIFICATION AND LIABILITY
The governing venue for any legal dispute shall be the state courts of New York. Vendor's total liability under this SOW shall not exceed $10,000,000.
